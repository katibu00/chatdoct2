Buying your Trash

We buy all types of recyclable trash through drop off at our collection centers or we pick up from you. Here are the list of trash we buy:

  •  PET Plastics (bailed, loose or in flakes form)
  •  LDPE, HDPE, PP, and PVC (packaging nylons)
  •  HDPE, PP (packaging containers)
  •  Pure Water Sachet
  •  UBC (aluminium cans)
  •  Iron Scraps (packaging containers)
  •  Shredded and unsharedded white papers (from schools and offices)
  •  BCC (Cartons)
  •  Housewares Plastic Scraps
  •  Old and empty sacks
  •  PVC Scraps (plumbing pipes, PVC ceiling sheet, expired I.d, and punched ATM cards)

Supply of Crushed and Recycled material

We supply LDPE, HDPE and PP in different colors either crushed or recycled form, while PVC is only in crushed form.

Trash Collection and Recycling Consultancy

We offer consultancy services on different form of trash, its collection, market value, recyclability and availability.

Corporate Services

We design a pick-up schedules for corporate bodies, provide customized trash bin for easier segregation and also train their cleaning staff on trash types for easier sorting at the point of generation.

Hands-on Trash Initiative (H.T.I)

Its an innovative participatory trash collection from the grassroots by leveraging on E.P.R, local Govt. and C.S.R to promote climate impact reduction actions for a resilient and safer environment for the next generation.

  •  Campaign on inclusive actions toward promoting clean and safe environment.
  •  Organizing general community clean-up.
  •  Govt policy that encourage sustainable waste management.
  •  Jingles and Radio programs on effect of climate change
