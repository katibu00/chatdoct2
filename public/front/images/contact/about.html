Who we are?

Trash and Eco Solution is a social enterprise which is making consistent and circumstantially appropriate move to influence the mind-set of people to drive an inclusive approach towards environmental sustainability action. A society with a mutually inclusive mindset to climate change action will find it easily to directly abundantly uncollected trash to collections agents/collection centers. A society having an environmentally conscious mindset, the collection agents will find it encouraging and seemly to collect and sort trash at the point of generation. After Trash to Resources: Innovatively accelerate trash collection from the grassroots and explore every option to create awareness and prevent accumulation of uncollected trash on the streets and drainages.
